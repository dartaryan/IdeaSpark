Quick Comparison

Direction Layout Density Best For Linear-like
1. Linear Classic Sidebar + Cards Medium Power users 5/5
2. Compact List Sidebar + List High Many items 4/5
3. Top Navigation Top nav + Full width Medium Familiar UX 2/5
4. Command-First Mini sidebar + Cmd Low Speed users 5/5
5. PRD Builder 50/50 Split Medium PRD building 3/5
6. Prototype Reveal Full screen Immersive Wow moment 3/5

My Recommendation:

Combine Directions 1 + 4 + 5 + 6:

  • Dashboard: Direction 1 (Linear Classic) for main idea management
  • Navigation: Direction 4 (Command-First) minimal sidebar + Cmd+K palette
  • PRD Builder: Direction 5 (Split View) for the conversation experience
  • Prototype: Direction 6 (Full Screen) for maximum "wow" factor
